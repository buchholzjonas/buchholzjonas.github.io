Because of the sight of the divine cow (Kāmadhenu) roaming about to provide everything that is needed for the sacrifices of the powerful ascetics, because of the gods who throng to receive the sacrificial offerings as their food, and because of its groves with many flowers that are not swarmed around by bees because they are cloaked by the billowing smoke from the oblations, Naimiśāraṇya truly is the dense and desirable celestial forest.
