After they had created each other in this way, they (Brahmā and Viṣṇu) became conceited; let those who are confused by the bad karma of previous births, thinking that these ones are the highest, be confused. All the great ones who have understood the true scriptures and reached grace realize that the primordial one who protected the gods by confining the poison to his throat and who has transcended the three excellent guṇas is the original cause for all.
