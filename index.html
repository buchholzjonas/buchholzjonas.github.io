We have spoken about the desirable greatness of the benefit-yielding place of the Lord of Puṇṇiyakōṭi.1 Now, O you who have practiced asceticism on Earth, we shall speak about the magnificence of Valampuri Viṉāyakaṉ upon the tall Atti hill.
